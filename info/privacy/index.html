Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to AnyVidWall, developed by BSR Studios. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use our website and the AnyVidWall Windows application.

2. The AnyVidWall Application (Desktop App)

AnyVidWall is designed to be a local, standalone application on your PC. We want to be completely transparent about how the app works:

  • No Personal Data Collection: The AnyVidWall desktop application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, account details, or hardware specifications.
  • Local File Processing: When you select a video or image to set as your wallpaper, the processing is done 100% locally on your computer. We do not upload your media files to any servers.
  • Offline Functionality: The core application works perfectly without an active internet connection.

3. Website & Analytics

When you visit our official download page, we may collect basic, non-personally identifiable information to ensure the site runs smoothly:

  • Download Tracker: We use a simple API (Application Programming Interface) to count the total number of AnyVidWall downloads. This API registers that a download occurred but does not track who downloaded it.
  • Standard Server Logs: Like most websites, our hosting provider may log standard data such as browser type, operating system, and the time of visit for security and performance monitoring.

4. Third-Party Services

AnyVidWall utilizes the open-source VLC Media Engine for video decoding. We do not integrate third-party ad networks or tracking SDKs inside the desktop application itself.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our app or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Any significant changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.
